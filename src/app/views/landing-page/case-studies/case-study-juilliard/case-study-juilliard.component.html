The Juilliard School

Leonard Bernstein Centennial Celebration

The goal was clear – to raise awareness and fill Carnegie Hall's 3,000 seats for the Juilliard Orchestra's most significant concert of the year. With thumb-stopping imagery capturing alumna Marin Alsop conducting and a teaser-filled promotional video revealing behind-the-scenes magic, the celebration came alive, complemented by nostalgic posts and insightful articles honoring the legacy of Lenny – one of the most iconic musical figures the world has ever known.
