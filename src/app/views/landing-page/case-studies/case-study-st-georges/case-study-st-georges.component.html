Engagement (Organic)

SGU Back to Campus 2022 Campaign

  • Goal(s): To build excitement around the return to campus after the pandemic shutdown
  • To welcome new students to SGU and Grenada and address frequently asked questions
  • To show the benefits of in-person learning
  • To showcase the vibrant and diverse campus community

Length: 6 weeks

    Posting frequency:
  • 1 feed post/day
  • live Instagram Stories updates from day-of events + UGC sharing
    KPIs and Results: Engagements: 17,693
  • Audience Growth: 1,038
  • Video Views: 147,201
  • Drive traffic to welcome website: 179 clicks
  • Drive traffic to informational blog posts and articles: 374 clicks

Platforms: Instagram

    Content:
  • Reels for “The Most Instagrammable Places on Campus,”
  • “How to Use the SGU Bus System,” advice on how new students can adjust to life at SGU,
  • “Welcome” in different languages,
  • “What to Expect on Your First Day” with SGA presidents,
  • “What to Pack,” milestones/rights of passage (white coat ceremonies)
  • “If I text you” trend, capitalizing on campus insider knowledge
  • Student, faculty, and staff features
  • Articles on what’s new on campus, how to adjust to life in Grenada, Grenada culture, student support services
